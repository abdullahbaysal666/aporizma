About Aporizma

Türkçesi aşağıdadır.

Aporizma is a growing collection of small, single-purpose web tools — subtitle utilities, PDF merge/split, QR codes, calculators, converters and more — built on one principle: your data is none of our business.

How it works

Every tool runs entirely in your browser with plain JavaScript. Files you drop and text you paste are processed on your device and never uploaded to any server. There are no accounts, no cookies, no analytics scripts. The site even works offline once loaded (it's an installable PWA).

Why free?

The tools are static pages that cost almost nothing to serve. The project grows a little every night — new tools are added continuously, ordered by what people actually search for. If a tool ever gains a paid tier, the free, private, in-browser core will stay free.

Open building blocks

Heavier tools embed open-source MIT libraries (pdf-lib, qrcode-generator), bundled with the site — no CDNs, no third-party requests. See our privacy policy for the full picture.
