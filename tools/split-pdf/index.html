Is my PDF uploaded?

No. Splitting happens entirely in your browser with the open-source pdf-lib library (bundled — no CDN). Safe even for contracts and private documents.

Range syntax?

Comma-separated pages and ranges: 1-3,7,10-12. Open ends work too: 5- means page 5 to the end, -4 means start through page 4. Order is preserved as typed, so 7,1 puts page 7 first.

Quality?

Pages are copied as-is — no re-compression, no quality loss.
