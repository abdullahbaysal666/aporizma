How it works

SRT → VTT: adds the WEBVTT header and switches timestamp commas to dots (00:00:01,000 → 00:00:01.000).

VTT → SRT: removes the header, NOTE and STYLE blocks, renumbers the cues and switches dots back to commas. Cue styling settings are dropped, text is kept as is.

Is my file uploaded?

No. The conversion runs entirely in your browser with JavaScript. Your subtitles never touch a server.
