When do you need this?

When subtitles appear too early or too late. If the words show up 2 seconds before they're spoken, shift by +2; if they lag 1.5 seconds behind, shift by -1.5.

Is my file uploaded?

No. Everything runs in your browser with JavaScript. Timestamps that would go below zero are clamped to 00:00:00.
