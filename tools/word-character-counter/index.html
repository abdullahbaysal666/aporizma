How is reading time calculated?

Based on an average silent reading speed of 200 words per minute. Faster or slower readers will vary.

How are sentences counted?

By counting groups of sentence-ending punctuation (., !, ?). Abbreviations like "Dr." or decimal numbers can occasionally throw off the count — treat it as an estimate.

Is my text uploaded anywhere?

No. Counting happens entirely in your browser with JavaScript. Nothing is sent to a server.
