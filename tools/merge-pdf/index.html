    Are my PDFs uploaded?

    No. Merging happens entirely in your browser using the open-source pdf-lib library (bundled — no CDN). Your documents never touch a server, which makes this safe even for contracts and private files.

    Any limits?

    Practical limit is your device's memory — hundreds of pages are fine. Password-protected PDFs can't be read; remove the password first.

    Does it keep quality?

    Yes — pages are copied as-is, with no re-compression.
