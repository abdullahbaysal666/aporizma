Paste or drop both subtitle files to merge.

When is this useful?

Combining dialogue with a separate signs/lyrics track, joining two halves of a film, or building a bilingual subtitle by merging two languages into one timeline.

How are conflicts handled?

Cues are sorted by start time and renumbered. Overlapping cues are kept as-is — most players display simultaneous cues stacked.

Is anything uploaded?

No. Everything runs in your browser; your files never leave your device.
