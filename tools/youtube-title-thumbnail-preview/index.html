Why preview?

YouTube truncates titles at roughly 2 lines on desktop cards and ~60-70 characters in mobile search. If your payoff is at the end of the title, viewers may never see it. Thumbnails also lose detail at feed size — what looks sharp in your editor can be unreadable at 210 pixels wide.

Is my thumbnail uploaded?

No. The image stays in your browser; nothing is sent anywhere.
