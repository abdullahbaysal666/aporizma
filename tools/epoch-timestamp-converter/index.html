How does unit detection work?

Timestamps with 10 digits or fewer are treated as seconds (valid up to the year 5138); 11–13 digits are treated as milliseconds. This covers every timestamp you'll encounter in practice.

Is anything sent to a server?

No. Conversion happens entirely in your browser with JavaScript — nothing is uploaded.
