Is my link uploaded anywhere?

No. The QR code is computed entirely in your browser (open-source MIT library, bundled — no CDN, no tracking). Your text never leaves your device.

What does error correction do?

QR codes carry redundancy so they scan even when partially covered or damaged. L tolerates ~7% damage, M ~15%, Q ~25%, H ~30%. Higher levels make the code denser — M is the everyday default.

Print quality?

The PNG is rendered pixel-perfect at your chosen size with a proper quiet zone. For print, pick 1024px.
