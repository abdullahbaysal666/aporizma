Is my photo uploaded?

No. Resizing uses your browser's canvas — the image never leaves your device. Safe for private photos and documents.

JPEG or WebP?

WebP is typically 25–35% smaller at the same visual quality and works everywhere modern. Choose JPEG only if the file must open in very old software.

Does it keep proportions?

Yes — the image is scaled to fit your maximum width, keeping the aspect ratio. Images already smaller than the limit are only re-compressed, never enlarged.
